What is the I3E?

The IESE Economic Uncertainty Index (I3E) is a daily indicator measuring economic uncertainty in some of the largest world economies.

To construct this index, we use the daily variation rates of the following economic and financial variables in each of the covered countries:

Domestic Equity Index

Domestic 10-year government bond prices

Domestic exchange rate

International Brent crude oil prices

The index reflects the magnitude of the daily growth rate fluctuations of these four variables. It is adjusted so that its average value is 100.

Index values below 100 indicate lower economic uncertainty compared to the average of the previous decade, while values above 100 indicate higher economic uncertainty.
